    At Demeter, we transform surplus into service—empowering communities to reclaim food, restore balance, and reduce waste.

    Rooted in Community

    Demeter connects local vendors, composting partners, and conscious consumers to build a sustainable food network. We believe nourishment should reach people—not landfills.

    Sign-in to Explore the Marketplace

    “Every loaf unclaimed, every fruit unsold, carries the potential to feed, to heal, to connect. Demeter is here to catch what would fall through the cracks—and place it back into human hands.”

    At Demeter, we believe food should nourish people—not landfills. Our platform makes it easy to turn surplus into service, and waste into opportunity.

    Built on Modern Tools, Powered by secure, scalable infrastructure, our platform simplifies food recovery and redistribution. From imperfect produce to expiring goods, every item claimed through Demeter helps close the loop.

    Whether you’re a local vendor, a conscious consumer, or a composting partner, Demeter gives you the tools to make a difference—one decision at a time.
