    • Frequently Asked Questions

    What is Demeter?

    Demeter is an online marketplace that connects individuals, vendors, and composters to reduce food waste by redistributing expiring or imperfect food items.

    How do I register?

    Click “Register” at the top right of any page. Choose whether you're signing up as a Customer, Vendor, or Composter, then follow the prompts.

    What types of food can I find on Demeter?

    You can find produce and packaged items close to expiry, with minor imperfections, or discounted surplus inventory from local vendors.

    How do composters use Demeter?

    Composters can register to browse unsold food from vendors nearby and request pickups for composting purposes.

    Is my payment secure?

    Yes. All payments are processed through trusted providers and protected via secure authentication through Amazon Cognito.
