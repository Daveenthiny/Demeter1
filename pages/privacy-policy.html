    Introduction

    Welcome to Demeter Marketplace. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our platform.

    Information We Collect

    • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, physical address, and user role (customer, vendor, composter).
    • Usage Data: Information about how you use our platform, including access times, pages viewed, and device information.

    How We Use Your Information

    • To create and manage your account.
    • To facilitate marketplace transactions.
    • To send administrative emails and service updates.
    • To improve our services and customer support.

    Sharing Your Information

    We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information with trusted third parties who perform services for us such as email delivery, and site hosting.

    Data Security

    We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Use our services at your own risk.

    Your Choices

    • You can update or delete your account information at any time through your account settings.
    • You can unsubscribe from marketing emails by following the opt-out instructions provided in the emails.

    Children's Privacy

    Our platform is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

    Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here and are effective immediately upon posting.

    Contact Us

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@demetermarketplace.com.
